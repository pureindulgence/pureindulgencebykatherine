Why Pure Indulgence?

A labour of love for our cake decorator-owner Katherine, ‘Pure Indulgence’ opened in Toronto, Canada in December 2020. Katherine is originally from Bangalore, India and this is where Pure Indulgence took its birth, in the year 2014. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for creating the moistest cakes you’ve ever eaten, Pure Indulgence offers a variety of sweet treats including (but not limited to) Indian style fresh cream cakes, classic cakes, cupcakes, macarons, chocolates and more. Using only the best ingredients and made with love, our bespoke baked goodies are a source of joy to many and we cannot wait to be part of your next celebration.
